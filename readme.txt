Halo Mod Quake 2

This mod is a Halo Themed Mod where we change weapons to behave similar to the common weapons in halo. It is a combination of halo reach and halo 4. We add the behaviors of different monsters such as thh Jackals, Elites, Grunts, promethean knights, Watcher.

New Weapons Added

*Battle Rifle
*Sniper Rifle
*Rail Gun
*Sticky Detonator
*Energy Sword


New Abilities

*Camouflage
*Armor Lock
*Jetpack
*Speed boost
*Shield Bubble

You also have a new companion that can heal you, attack enemies, give you ammo, has voice lines, and can use a shield.
