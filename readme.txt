Halo Mod Quake 2

This mod is a Halo Themed Mod where we change weapons to behave similar to the common weapons in halo. It is a combination of halo reach and halo 4. We add the behaviors of different monsters such as the Jackals, Elites, Grunts, promethean knights, Watcher.

Wave System
Throughout the run on the map the wave will begin and slowly start ramping up in how fast monsters appear.

New Weapons Added

*Battle Rifle - Machine Gun
*Sniper Rifle - Rocket Launcher
*Rail Gun - Rail Gun
*Sticky Detonator - Grenade Launcher
*Energy Sword - Hyper Blaster


New Abilities

*Camouflage - increased dodge chance
*Armor Lock - stuns you in place but no damage taken
*Jetpack - super high jump
*Speed boost - crazy speed increase
*Shield Bubble - restores shield

New Monster Behaviour
Grunts(Soldiers) - can explode on contact
Jackals(Infantry) - Speed increased to 300
Elite(Gladiator) - Increased health to 400hp
Watchers(Flyers) - shoot rockets and fly but health is 10 hp
Promethean Knights(Tank) - can fly and have 200hp

You also have a new companion that can heal you, attack enemies, give you ammo, can follow you around, and can use a shield.
